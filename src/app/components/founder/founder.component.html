About the Founder - COACH.H

Coach.H is a social mobiliser & a mixed martial artist from the East End of London, (Tower Hamlets). Growing up through adverse poverty and social delinquencies, Coach.H developed a passion to create progressive changes on a global and local scale by empowering hearts and minds through education and exercise. Now you may be questioning what (H) stands for. There have been numerous discussions regarding this topic. Some have said it stands for humanity and while others have said hero, who knows perhaps it may remain a mystery

The viewpoint that I embrace in life is with a deep understanding that youth and community work is not a 9-5 job but rather a way of life. In order to bring the best out of young people and the wider community, we must be willing to give back in order to empower all those around us.
