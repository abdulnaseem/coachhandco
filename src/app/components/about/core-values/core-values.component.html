Values and Beliefs

At its core, our motto is to Educate, Empower, Exercise

“The ability to empower an individual or a group can be best fostered through two streams, education and exercise.”

Coach.H

By fostering empowerment through our body and using education as our wings, we hope to create the notion of self-belief. Taking from the butterfly effect, we believe that by empowering our youth through these means, we provide them with opportunities and a long-lasting relationship with the community and a renewed outlook on life.

Before one can ‘fly’ we believe that our youth should strive to complete the following core values:

self_improvement
